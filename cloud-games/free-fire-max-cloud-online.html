Custom Key Mapping for Easy Gameplay

One of the best features of playing Free Fire Max on Easyfun is the fullscreen mode, which allows you to fully enjoy the graphical improvements on a larger screen for a higher-quality experience. But just using your mouse isn't the best way to play. That's why Easyfun introduced custom key mapping, where you can choose your preferred keys to activate in-game actions like looting, changing weapons, using medkits, shooting, and more.

By setting these keys around the WASD movement keys, you can improve your reaction time and execute actions quickly, no matter the combat situation. To help you find the perfect setup, the key editing mode can be used while playing the game, making layout changes easy. Plus, with the key layout toggle button, you can display the key layout on screen, making it simple to get used to your new controls.

Make Use of Combo Keys

Another helpful feature is combo keys, which can enhance your survival. For example, if you enter the key layout customization and drag the crouch button close to the aiming button, then save. Then you can enter Easyfun's key editing mode. Right-click on the screen and drag one click action buttons onto the crouch and scope buttons. Set a single keyboard key, like Q, to trigger both actions at once, so you can execute both faster by spamming one button. This will allow for quicker cover aiming. You can also set up similar combos for jumping and shooting. Spamming one button in close combat will make you a harder target to hit while still being able to shoot.

Low Lag for Better Reaction

As you may have noticed, Free Fire Max brings more detailed environments and smoother gameplay. However, when playing on a cloud platform like Easyfun, this can lead to lag if you have low bandwidth or high ping. Lag can cause issues like firing your weapons too late, repeatedly interacting with the environment (like doors, looting, and movement), which could put you in dangerous situations unintentionally.

If you're experiencing these issues, there's a simple trick you can try. Look for the HD button on the Easyfun operation bar and press it. You'll find different stream quality settings there. For smoother gameplay, make sure to set it to "Smooth" before starting each session. This works similarly to lowering video quality while streaming, reducing buffering time and, in this case, lowering lag. In addition, make sure to play while staying close to your Wi-Fi router and restart both your device and router every few days to keep everything in top condition.
