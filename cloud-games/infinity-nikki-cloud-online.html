Switch Devices Quickly

Sometimes, you may need to switch devices while playing. For instance, if you're waiting in a queue, you must keep the game tab active to avoid being kicked out. This can interfere with using your device for other tasks. Here's an easy solution:

Log in to your Easyfun account on both devices. Use the device you're not currently using to wait in the queue. Once the game loads, without closing the session on the first device, simply load the game on the second device where you'd prefer to play. The session on the first device will automatically become inactive, allowing you to continue seamlessly on the new device without losing your queue position.

If you're already logged into the game on one device, you won't need to log in again on the second device. The session continues exactly where you left off!

To do this, you'll need an Easyfun account. Creating one is free, and you'll get 6 hours of free playtime daily.
