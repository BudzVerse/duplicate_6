Play MONOPOLY GO! On Your Browser with Easyfun

MONOPOLY GO!, developed by Scopely, brings a sophisticated and modern twist to the classic board game. In this strategy-based game, you have access to a variety of tools to build your empire, all while enjoying a dynamic and visually engaging experience. The game's vivid visuals and interactive gameplay ensure players are immersed in a daily gaming adventure. Challenge your friends, take on entertaining quests, and explore themed boards that add a creative spin to the timeless Monopoly experience. Each turn offers unexpected surprises, making every game session unique and exciting. Best of all, you can play MONOPOLY GO! directly in your web browser, no downloads or installations required. Jump in and start building your fortune today!

Login for Safe Progress

When you start the game, you'll be presented with login options. The best choice is to log in using social media accounts, like Facebook. This allows you to connect and compete with friends, share dice, visit their lands, demolish their properties, and enjoy many other fun features.

If you play as a guest, you'll miss out on these activities. More importantly, your progress won't be saved the next time you load the game. This is because on Easyfun, you're using a shared device. These devices are cleaned after 5 minutes of you stop playing, and any unsaved data will be wiped if you haven't logged in.

Additionally, playing as a guest and later syncing your data to a social account can cause issues. For example, your profile might get stuck on the device, allowing the next player to access your game account. So the best thing you can do is login and play.

Never Let the Dice Go to Waste

Dice are the main action currency in the game. As long as you have them, you can roll and keep playing. Once you run out, you'll need to wait for them to regenerate. However, if you accumulate more dice than your maximum limit, the auto-regeneration stops, wasting potential dice income.

To avoid this, make sure to use your dice regularly before they hit the maximum limit. On Easyfun, you have a 6-hour window to log in multiple times throughout the day and spend the dice as they regenerate. The best part? You don't need to install anything! Just hit the play button and start playing directly in your browser.
